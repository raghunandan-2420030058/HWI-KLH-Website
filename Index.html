My Projects

Automated Temperature-Controlled Fan

An energy-efficient, low-cost cooling system that automatically activates a fan when ambient temperature exceeds a preset threshold.

How It Works:

An LM35 sensor feeds temperature data to an Arduino Uno. If the temperature crosses the set point, the Arduino signals a transistor, turning on a fan. When the temperature drops, the fan turns off, saving power.

Key Achievement:

Recognized at our college expo for its practicality and innovative approach to energy saving.
